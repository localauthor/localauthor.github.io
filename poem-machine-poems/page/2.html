04-22-2022

the difficulty of resemblance

a vicar grin, medicinal man

exalted of another, impressive

that blood, steele, most general delicacy

drawn, sifted, serving,

subcoracoid or mediterranean

once too assumed, curious, haemorrhoidal

A long part with capillaries, geld

sesquioxide breath, a wise thing

"So improved, incessently, time

larder, coextended, adducing

confusion, graven sweetness

good ages, one vent measure, sense year fern

is graduated, or the whole, the learning

shells grace abundance, dog-rose.

look for, find, cannot


trod maid of field

of her touch and find, of look

sovereign upon me, all

soundless amber


encore, beheld, all brews thine

were not in upon

any distant spices

while north gone white


Industrious Repudiate, as if now had itself


Troubled pools

intellectual agonized, of brimming nosegays

flits, fate keepsakes

a stream, a sea, of grave


a frantic little daily

worshipping ajar

rugged that regret, soul's reached
